How to change iphone HEIF camera settings

#iphone #images #camera #heif

To change your iPhone's HEIF settings, go to Settings > Camera > Formats, and select "Most Compatible" to capture photos in JPEG format instead of HEIF. This will ensure that new photos are saved in a more widely compatible format.
